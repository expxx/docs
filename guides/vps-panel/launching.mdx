## Launching the Panel
To launch the panel, head on over to your product page at https://billing.createnow.gg/clientarea.php. Once you click on your service, it should look something like the following.

![Service Info](/images/service_info.png)

Click on the big green "Open Control Panel" button. Once there, you've successfully launcehd the control panel. You'll be immediately brought to your Service, where you can [Rebuild the system](/guides/vps-panel/rebuild.mdx) if you haven't already!
